How it Works

AS Academy was founded in 2021 with one goal in mind: to develop study methods that will help as many students as possible achieve academic excellence. Students can take advantage of both the centre and online website, to aid their learning journey.

Centre tutoring

Our in-person centre is currently based in London, here we deliver tutoring services for students between 7 and 16. Call us at 07868 886152, if you're interested in centre tuition for your child.


Online tutoring

Through our online website, you can book online lessons, courses, purchase membership plans and keep track of your child's progress through emails from tutors.
